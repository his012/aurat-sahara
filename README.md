# Aurat Sahara — عورت سہارا

**Empowering Pakistani women by recognizing 
their skills and connecting them to official 
certification pathways.**

---

## 💡 The Problem

Pakistan's NAVTTC runs a free government 
program called RPL (Recognition of Prior 
Learning) — designed to certify people who 
learned their skills informally, at home, 
from family, or through years of practice.

In all of FY 2023-24, only **3,700 people** 
were certified across the entire country.

We shared this with our classmates. Nobody 
had heard of it. We told our professor. 
He hadn't heard of it either.

If university students don't know — what 
chance does a woman in Hyderabad who has 
been tailoring for 20 years have of knowing 
her skills can be officially recognized, 
**for free?**

That gap is what Aurat Sahara was built 
to bridge.

---

## 🌸 What is Aurat Sahara?

Aurat Sahara is an AI-powered civic tech 
web platform that helps Pakistani women 
document, verify, and certify their 
informal skills — and connects them 
directly to Pakistan's official RPL 
certification pathway.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌐 **Multilingual** — English, Urdu, 
  Roman Urdu
- 🤖 **AI Chat Assistant** — Guides women 
  through certificate application in 
  their own language
- 📜 **Hunar Sanad** — Verified digital 
  skill certificate with unique 
  verification link
- 🔔 **Notifications** — Real-time updates 
  on application status
- 🛡️ **Admin Panel** — Review, approve, 
  or decline applications
- 🔗 **NAVTTC RPL Link** — Direct pathway 
  to free government certification
- 🎤 **Voice Input** — English and Roman 
  Urdu speech to text support

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. User selects their language
2. Signs up with email
3. AI chatbot collects skill information 
   through friendly conversation
4. Admin reviews and approves or declines
5. Approved users receive a verified 
   Hunar Sanad certificate
6. Certificate links to NAVTTC RPL for 
   official government certification

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of skilled Pakistani women — 
tailors, cooks, tutors, embroiderers — 
have no formal proof of their expertise. 
Pakistan's free RPL certification system 
exists but is completely unknown. 
Aurat Sahara makes it accessible — 
across all of Pakistan.
